Abot-Kamay is a capstone innovation created by a team of Electronics Engineering students from Batangas State University. Our mission is to promote inclusive and equitable education for the visually impaired students by providing them a refreshable braille display intended for home-based learning.

In a world that relies heavily on visual learning, we believe that every learner deserves equal access to information, no matter their ability or location. Through Abot-Kamay, we harness technology to touch lives, one Braille cell at a time.
